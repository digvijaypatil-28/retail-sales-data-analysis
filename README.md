# Retail Sales Data Analysis

## Business Problem
Retail businesses need to understand customer behavior, seasonal demand, and product performance to optimize marketing, inventory, and pricing strategies.  
This project analyzes historical retail transaction data to uncover actionable insights that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Objectives
- Identify high-value customer segments
- Analyze sales trends across time and seasons
- Evaluate product category performance
- Understand pricing and quantity-based purchasing behavior
- Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## Dataset Overview
- Transaction-level retail sales data
- Includes customer demographics, product categories, pricing, and quantities
- Time-based data enabling trend and seasonal analysis

---

## Key Business Questions Answered
- Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
- When should inventory be increased or reduced?
- Which product categories drive revenue vs volume?
- Are customers price-sensitive or premium-oriented?
- Do bulk buyers contribute significantly to revenue?

---

## Tools & Technologies

## Business Recommendations
- Prioritize high-value customer segments in marketing campaigns.
- Increase inventory for high-demand categories before peak seasons.
- Apply premium pricing to low price-sensitive categories.
- Introduce loyalty or bulk-purchase incentives for high-value customers.

## Limitations & Future Scope
